MY PROPER REPLY


"Life is getting tougher day by day, the one who never quits make it easier someday"

Recently, one of my friends asked me for motivation. And i wasn't able to reply properly, mostly because I don't give motivational speeches or engage in such activities.
But now,I think that there is something I need to share.
OH SORRY! don't think that it's a motivational article. Consider it as my proper reply.

People today experience mental exhaustion more frequently than in the past.
hmm..... I really don't know why. But when i give it a thought, maybe it's because people are doing the same thing everyday. Nothing creative is happening. Just going to college/ work , wasting too much time in social media's, sacrifising your sleep for movies/series, overthinking etc......
OKAY LEAVE IT. Now let me ask you something,
What happened to the creative part?  or What happen to your passion that you always wanted to follow ?

Yaa !! i know too busy, right?
My friend was seeking confidence to take decisions, thinking that something might go wrong.
OK WAIT, Making a decision does not imply that it must always be the right one. Take a successful football player as an example; he makes important judgments while on the field. It works and doesn't work occasionally. It depends on his level of experience. He will fail trying, but that's good since experience isn't something that can be earned quickly. Eventually, he will be in charge of his team. Life is just like that; in order to succeed, we must gain knowledge and experience. Experience is gained by going through it, not by reading about it.
So simply take a chance, do what you want, and always be engaged.

There isn't a single person who can motivate you better than yourself. How hard I try or someone else try, Its your thing to be motivated, not mine. Its funny seeing Youtube thumbnails like "This is the best motivation", "This is what you need", "Ten motivational tips",lol..... Trust me that isn't motivation, They are just boosters that works for 1 or 2 days. The real motivation is inside you that continuously tells you "not to GIVE UP".

For me, there is a particular incident that constantly motivates me. It's a bit tough to describe, when I was in the Xth grade, I basically created a fuss and let my parents down that too in front of my class teacher. Even if those memories make me crazy, teary, furious, and aggressive, it's good because they only KEEPS MY SPIRIT BURNING.

I believe it's okay at times to feel depressed since doing so is exactly how you strive to get to know yourself, ask questions, and discover solutions.

Please consider this to be "MY PROPER REPLY" even if it is a bit lengthy (sorry) and seems more like a motivational article.

"You dont need to seek motivation , Its already inside you. Just let it burn"



BLOG : 3


SORRY AND THANKYOU


Sorry and Thankyou, these words are getting so rare nowadays. Most of you wont agree to this. But just think about it, people of this era are more egotistical and don't give a thing about others, "Why should I apologize?,let him/her do it.","Is there any reason to show grattitude,Do I really need to ?" - these are so common now.
You might think that I'm talking bullshit ,Sometimes I do feel the same :)lol.
OK now let me be frank, I'm a victim of this egotistical era and a person who carry pain(more like regrets) of having the above mentioned attitude.
So thats the exact reason why I'm writing this blog.

Days back, when I was in high school I was a boy who don't give a thing about others . For me its just me and only me, Like I never tried to understand others ,Used to take people lightly, more materialistic...Ya, so Boring. So back then, I had a friend in my tution named Anandhu, THEY had a seperate team .They weren't my type and I never considered them to be my actual friends, For me they were just temporary guys with whom I need to stick for a while.
Day's passed and I still gave them my ATTITUDE, But we really started to enjoy moments together. WHAT I DID WAS, I JUST BECAME A FAKE PERSON. Almost an year passed, And everything changed. I started to like them, especially him - ANANDHU - mainly because he had a beautiful soul , Even if i wasn't treating him properly he did treated me so nicely.
I always wanted to say sorry for the way I behaved, But my ego never let me apologize.

HaHa... now you guys might think -- "So what ?, I can apologize now". You Know I really wish to, but after a few months I got a call from my friend's mom saying that he is "NO MORE". Booooommm .... He committed suicide. I dont know why, But he got real problems thats the only thing I know. And I never tried to help him, not even once. THE NEWS HIT ME SO BADLY, I was stuck for a while Then I cried and with the sadly pounding heart, I said "SORRY".
And atlast the only thing remained was regret.."PURE REGRET".

After this incident I always did apologized , almost to everything... Hey, I'm not telling you to GO APOLOGIZE ALWAYS.But when you feel to, don't hesitate because sometimes you wont get a second chance.
The one important thing He taught me was , "Showing gratitude is not repaying debt, Its just sharing love".

“There are no regrets in life, just lessons.”
-Jennifer Aniston

SOLUTION



Hey... I was a bit busy with my exams, thats why I wasn't able to write for a while.
Last day I had a situation like, One of my friends was so upset,because he was badly scolded by his father for investing money in stocks.And guess what ? I had to calm him down :[ by talking bullshits].
So this blog is all about my response/answer towards the question Why parents are getting too tensed while we say "I started to earn money", or if we say "I started a small online business" or "I started to invest in stocks/trading".

First of all I'm not a greedy or a selfish person who loves money more than anything, to speak on this topic. But I really love making money, or doing businesses, It feels so good when I do something productive and earn real cash or using our brain to create passive income.
Okay, now let me come to the topic, Most middle class families react so tensed or afraid when a topic like this comes up. I know the exact reason why they react like that, It's because fear, fear of spoiling their childs future / studies.

My parents don't support any of this, my father always scolds me for doing such stuffs - like earning money. Most parents want their child to have a degree and work for a company or an organization , And after gathering more experience shift to another company which provides you with more salary. And at last retire as a person working on a high position so that "We, the children can make our future generations safe".

Yea, I know these are all problems "What about the solutions ?". It's funny, I don't have a proper solution for this :). We can't change their attitudes, It's so difficult. But I think rather than changing them, It's better we change. Change means adjusting ourselves in a way which we satisfy our parents and at the same time we do what we love to, You know what I mean ;).. Hey!! Just Remember -- Get a degree or achieve a skill that will be handy in the future, not to satisfy your current needs. I know I'm not a successful person to give advice or motivations, As I said before I am just an ordinary student chasing big dreams.And these are all my findings/conclusion based on several researches.
I don't care If I fail doing something that I love to. You Know why ?, because "I TRIED".

I know, Most won't find this blog that interesting, but it's something that I felt sharing.
And one more thing - I have a question for you guys,
"Why do you want to make money? " - Answer me if you can.

COMPROMISE-NEVER



Hello,
I wasn't able to write for a while because I had Semester exams.
But now I'm free.....
Lot of things happend, I was into deep things and was so depressed of it. I really lost confidence and was so tired. But then I realized that there is no need for me to stay like this, I have been through such situations before.

Problem is that I compromised my policy of being truthful that I promised myself not to break.
Ya, everything went wrong. But It's okay, I consider it as a phase in my life (Like a chapter).
We should not compromise ourselves no matter what . We are who we are, dare to face the problems being yourself. I always wanted people to love me, rather not hate me. but the truth is that we can't make everyone like us or think favourably of us.
The only thing that we all should remember is that we should be truthful towards ourselves, whenever something goes wrong ~ ask yourselves, was it right ? If not go make it right or apologize for what you did. Most of the time you wont get an second chance but if you get one, NEVER WASTE IT.

Ok now leave such things,I would like to share something thats currently in my mind. When I first started writing blog I was full of Energy and contents/subjects, But now when I give it a thought I feel like I don't have anything to share / or like my memory is completely drained, And also not having enough time to write blogs. People say that if you have something in your mind which can be executed or more like productive stuffs, They say just start it . Starting such idea is the only difficult part and else will come through the flow. But I comletely disagree with that , Starting is difficult then maintaining is much more difficult and at last getting successful is much much much more dificult.
Now I made my mind, I wont strop writing blogs because atleast I need to complete something that I had started.

2022 taught me that you can modify yourself but never compromise yourself. Life is a oneway track so never regret for what you do. Be truthful and honest in any situation. And one more thing "If your deeds are good, Then people will definitely stay with you. Not so quick but definitely someday".
